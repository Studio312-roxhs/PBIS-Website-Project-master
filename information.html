FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PBIS?

This is a program designed to acknowledge and reward you for doing the right thing. By Being RoxStarrs ( Safe, Thrive, Accountable, Respectful, Responsible students) you can help make this school a better place, and enjoy your high school experience.

What can I do to get points?

Well, to put it simply - just do the right thing. Go to class on time, be polite to everyone, have patience with everyone, and take responsibility for your actions. Also, picking up a piece of trash in the hallway or holding a door open for someone is always nice to do. If you do something nice for someone and an RHS adult sees it, they may ask for your name to give you points. Just check out the comic on the home page .

What can I do with my points?

You can attend monthly special events like the Hot Chocolate Bar party in November, the Ice Cream social kick-off in September, and Movie days. Coming soon we are planning some other ways to use your points, like RHS SWAG, Free entry to schoolwide events that you would have to normally pay for, and more. Tell your Class representative in your Community meeting your ideas. The PBIS team will be meeting with them during the Community Meetings to plan events, or you can click here to submit an idea.

How can I find my points?

Great question! Go to your INFINITE campus,
log in to your SDP account, go to the MESSAGE center,
and select the PBIS Icon

Do points expire after every month?

At this point in the year, they do not expire. However, this may change - so make sure you listen for announcements and check the announcements section on the home page.

Depending upon the grade will you recieve different priviledges?

That is something to consider for grades 9-11. For seniors, we are working some priveledges when the weather gets warmer. Check back and listen for announcements to learn what they are.

What is the point of PBIS/RoxSTARRS?

You earn points for being a RoxStarr. That means you come to school & class on time, do great work, respect the place and the people around you, and take responsibility for your actions. The points build up and you can participate in monthly events, earn privileges, and coming soon - get some swag.

You have quesions, we have answers!
