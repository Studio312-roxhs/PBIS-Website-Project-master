What's Happening Now

This is the first year for our RoxSTARRS PBIS program. We hold monthly events to celebrate your success! The PBIS team meets regularly to plan the events and come up with ideas that you will hopefully like. The team is made up of mostly teachers and the administration team, but we want your voice too! We have started Community Meeting periods (aka Advisory) to ask students what they would like. Community Meetings are held once a week, in an effort to help make Roxborough High School the best it can be, give you time to make new friends, catch up with teachers, and laugh a bit. Check the RHS Instagram page to see how much fun the events were!
