What's Happening Now

This is the first year for our RoxSTARRS PBIS program. We hold monthly events to celebrate your successes. The PBIS team meets regularly to plan the events and come up with ideas that hopefully you will like. The team is mostly teachers and the administration team, but we want your voice too. We have had a few meetings during the Community Meeting periods aka Advisory to ask students what they would like. Community Meetings are also held once weekly so we can share ways to help make Roxborough High School the best it can be, and give you time to make new friends, catch up with teachers, and laugh a bit. Check the RHS Instagram page to see how much fun the events were!
