FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PBIS?

This is a program designed to acknowledge and reward you for doing the right thing. By Being RoxStarrs ( Safe, Thrive, Accountable, Respectful, Responsible students) you can help make this school a better place, and enjoy your high school experience!

You earn points for being a RoxStarr. That means you come to school & class on time, do great work, respect the place and the people around you, and take responsibility for your actions. Your points build up and allow you to participate in monthly events, earn rewards, and more coming soon!

What can I do to get points?

Well, to put it simply - just do the right thing! Go to class on time, be polite, have patience, and take responsibility for your actions. Also, picking up a piece of trash in the hallway, or holding a door open for someone is always a nice thing to do. If you do something nice for someone and an RHS adult sees it, they may ask for your name to give you points.

What can I do with my points?

You can attend monthly special events like the Hot Chocolate Bar party in November, the Ice Cream social kick-off in September, and Movie days. Coming soon we are planning some other ways to use your points, like RHS SWAG, Free entry to schoolwide events that you would have to normally pay for, and more. Tell your Class representative in your Community meeting your ideas. The PBIS team will be meeting with the class reps. during Advisory to plan events, or you can click hereto submit an idea yourself.

How can I find my points?

Great question!

  • Go to your INFINITE CAMPUS
  • Login to your SDP account
  • Navigate to the Message Center
  • Select the PBIS Icon

Do points expire after every month?

At this point in the year, they do not expire. However, this may change - so make sure you listen for announcements and check the home page.

Depending upon the grade, Will you recieve different priviledges?

For grades 9-11, we have been working hard to get some things together for you. More information will be announced soon. For seniors, we have a few things plnned when it gets warmer. Check back and listen for announcements to learn what they are.
